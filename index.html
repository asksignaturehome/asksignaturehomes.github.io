The Market

Why Dubai, Why Now

A unique combination of tax advantages, high yields, strong capital appreciation, and lifestyle quality — in a city with no income tax.

0% Capital gains tax on property sales
8–12% Average gross rental yield across key areas
10yr Golden Visa with AED 2M+ property investment
#3 Global city for millionaire inflows in 2024
  • Freehold Ownership for All Nationalities
    International buyers own Dubai property outright in designated freehold zones — no time limits, no restrictions, full title deed in your name.
  • Off-Plan Payment Plans — No Mortgage Needed
    Pay as little as 10% down and spread payments over 3–5 years directly with the developer. Access premium properties with far less upfront capital.
  • Strong & Consistent Capital Growth
    Dubai property values rose 19.8% in 2024. Prime areas show 4–6% annual appreciation over a decade, backed by government infrastructure investment.
  • Remote Transactions Fully Supported
    You do not need to visit Dubai to buy. We handle developer registration, DLD paperwork, and payment plan setup entirely on your behalf.
