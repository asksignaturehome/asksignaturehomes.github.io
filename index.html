    International buyers own Dubai property outright in designated freehold zones — no time limits, no restrictions, full title deed in your name.
  • Off-Plan Payment Plans (No Mortgage Needed)
    Pay as little as 10% down and spread payments over 3–5 years directly with the developer. Access premium properties with far less upfront capital.

    Dubai property values rose 19.8% in 2024. Prime areas show 4–6% annual appreciation over a decade, backed by government infrastructure investment and population growth.

    You do not need to visit Dubai to buy. We handle developer registration, DLD paperwork, and payment plan setup. Hundreds of international buyers complete transactions entirely remotely.
